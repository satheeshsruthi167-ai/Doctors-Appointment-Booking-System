ABOUT US

ABOUT THE HOSPITAL

MediCare Hospital is a new-age, tertiary care, multi-specialty healthcare facility that is designed to offer efficiency and effectiveness in its clinical and diagnostic services. Our focus is on creating an environment that is conducive to providing proficient healthcare services by bringing together modern technology, a team of supremely talented healthcare professionals and an honest professional ethic. We are also committed to empower our patients and their caregivers with the power of choice. We consider it our duty to give our patients easy to understand information and maintain absolute transparency to help them make well-informed and well-planned decisions. We are equipped to provide holistic solutions for ever-evolving patient needs owing to our wide scale of services and a well-trained team of doctors, nurses, technicians and management professionals.

At MediCare Hospital, we are devoted to providing the best for our patients. Our people, our facilities, our policies and even our aspirations are all driven by our commitment to care.

OUR VISION

To provide the highest quality of health care services to every patient who comes to us trusting us with their health, utilizing the best of our God-given abilities.

OUR MISSION

MediCare Hospital is committed to provide the best of quality health care services and facilities, complying to all standards of evidence based medical and surgical care, with due consideration of social and regulatory requirements.
